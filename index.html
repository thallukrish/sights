Sights

Intelligent Product Monitoring & AI-Powered Shopping Insights

Project Overview

Sights is a comprehensive web application designed to help consumers make informed purchasing decisions by monitoring Amazon products, tracking price changes, analyzing trends, and providing AI-powered intelligent recommendations.

Purpose: This platform will integrate Amazon Associate Program links to help users discover the best deals while supporting our development through ethical affiliate commissions.

Price Tracking

Monitor price changes across thousands of products with historical trend analysis and alert notifications.

AI-Powered Insights

LLM integration for intelligent product analysis, natural language insights, and smart recommendations.

Market Intelligence

Comprehensive analytics on product categories, brand comparisons, and seasonal trends.

Real-time Monitoring

Automated monitoring system that tracks availability, prices, and promotional offers.

Amazon Associate Integration

Ethical Monetization: This platform will implement Amazon Associate Program links transparently, helping users discover great products while generating modest affiliate commissions to support ongoing development and hosting costs.

Implementation Strategy

  • Transparent Disclosure: Clear affiliate link disclosures on all pages
  • User Value First: Focus on providing genuine value through accurate data and AI insights
  • Quality Curation: Recommend products based on AI analysis, not commission rates
  • Link Management: Automated affiliate link insertion with proper attribution
  • Performance Tracking: Monitor click-through rates and conversion analytics

Content Strategy

  • AI-generated product buying guides and comparisons
  • Weekly deal roundups and price drop alerts
  • Category-specific recommendation articles
  • Market trend analysis and seasonal insights
  • Community-driven reviews with AI sentiment analysis
